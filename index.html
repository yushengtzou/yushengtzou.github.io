On-Going Projects

3D Face Model Reconstruction for Surgical Simulation and Visualization

This project explores the integration of "NOC: High-Quality Neural Object Cloning with 3D Lifting of Segment Anything" for the reconstruction of 3D facial models aimed at enhancing surgical simulation and visualization. It targets the advancement of pre-operative planning and training in cosmetic and reconstructive facial surgery through the development of a highly accurate, detailed, and realistic 3D face modeling system. By leveraging the novel neural object cloning technique combined with 3D lifting, our approach transforms 2D facial images into textured 3D models, significantly improving the fidelity and realism of surgical simulations.
